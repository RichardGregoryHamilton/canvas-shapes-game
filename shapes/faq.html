Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this game?

Answer: This is an arcade-style game. Different shapes will randomly appear on the board. Your task is to quickly identify the shape. At first, you are given quite a bit of time to make a decision. As this is an arcade game, it gets progressively harder and harder. The time you have to think will decrease. You will have to think on your feet.

Question 2: How do you earn coins?

Answer: Coins are earned as a result of your hard work. They can be used to unlock new features. For every five points you earn, you are awarded 1 coin. Coins can also be purchased.

Question 3: Do you have to create an account?

Answer: No. Best of all, this game can be played without signing up.

Question 4: Do you have to pay to play this game?

Answer: Shapes Game is free to play. However, you can choose to purchase coins to help you unlock features faster

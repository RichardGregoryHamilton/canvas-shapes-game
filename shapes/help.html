How To Play

This is an arcade-style game where you are presented with a shape. Your objective is to determine which shape appears on the screen

The above shape is a rectangle, so you would click on the rectangle button

The above shape is a circle, so you would click on the circle button

As you level up, you will be presented with many different shapes. This only adds to the challenge as you have to quickly determine what shape shows up. At high levels, you will need lightning fast hand-eye coordination.

Controls
